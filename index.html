Welcome to UMPest, or UMass Pest!

This is an expanded site inspired by the current university page on pest control! Not affiliated with UMass Amherst. Hover on "Pests" to get started, or read about the site!

The dorms (and many academic buildings) of UMass Amherst often become plagued by pests, particularly during the fall and winter seasons. If you're confused about what you're up against, or need help removing these critters, you've come to the right place! The most common invaders of UMass, namely cockroaches, stinkbugs, Asian lady beetles, and fruit flies, each have their own dedicated page in the navigator above.
