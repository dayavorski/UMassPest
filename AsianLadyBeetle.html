Know Your Enemy!

Asian Lady Beetles are very similar in appearance to ladybugs, with a couple key differences. Asian lady beetles can range in color, while ladybugs are almost exclusively solid red. Asian Lady Beetles tend to have more spots, potentially as many as 19!

Manage This Pest!

Capture and release outside, or suck them up with a vacuum to prevent the beetle from releasing a sticky substance on your walls.
